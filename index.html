About Us

Engineering the Circular Future from France.

SKB CORPORATION is a French industrial equipment company delivering modular solutions for the circular economy — combining engineering excellence with global project execution capability.

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in industrial equipment supply, renewable energy project development, and circular economy solutions — with a track record spanning multiple continents.

We don't just sell concepts. We deliver equipment. From pyrolysis plants to containerized microgrids, our focus is on modular, scalable, and proven industrial solutions that turn waste into value.
